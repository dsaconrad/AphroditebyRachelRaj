Don Bosco's Kurla is where I pursued a Major in Media Communications with a specialization in Advertising. It is here that I discovered my passion for pastry as a manifestation of my profound love for art.
Upon graduating, I went on to study at Le Cordon Bleu. This world renowned institution taught me to channel my love for edible art into delectable Chocolate treasures and intricately crafted Sugar masterpieces.
With professors guiding me atevery step, I decided to hone my creativity by mastering the art of French Pastries.
With every passing day and in the company of incredibly talented Chefs, I learnt that you are limited only by your imagination and that anything is possible if you have vision.
Gordon Ramsay once said “To be a great Chef you need to train with great Chefs." And Cordon Bleu has been a great influence in helping me launch my dream — Aphrodite.
In Greek mythology, Aphrodite is the Goddess of love, lust, beauty, passion with a flair for the theatrics. And that’s exactly what me and my brand are all about.
My Art pieces at Aphrodite are not just simple/ordinary desserts, it's LOVE, PASSION & ART summed up into something as warm and comforting as a slice of layered Opera, a spoon of creamy Crème brûlée or a handcrafted piece of gourmet chocolate made with Fine Belgian chocolate.
Call or Drop in a Message and be witness to something remarkably EXOTIC and PASSIONATE, that will leave you lusting for more.
